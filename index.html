DrupalCon Vienna 2025 - Custom Schedule Builder

Usage Instructions

  • Browse and filter sessions and events to find the ones you want to attend.
  • Click on a session to select it.
  • Use the "Save Selections" button to save your selections to your browser's local storage for later visits.
  • Use the "Add to Calendar" button to add all selected sessions directly to your calendar application.
  • Alternatively, use the "Download as ICS" button to download your personalized schedule in ICS format.
